What is DIG THE DATA?

Dig The Data Is a treasure hunt event to display the creativity and critical thinkings of freshers. It's a fresher exclusive event where they perform in various stages and ultimately stand tall on the podium. In this event, freshers explore the whole campus looking for clues and solve problems while exploring various details about our lovely campus and get familiarised with it. The previous seasons displayed the enthusiasm and the energy it provides, creating a wonderful and everlasting memory.
